MEET EVE

×

Sunswift eVe is the fifth generation solar car designed and built by the University of New South Wales Solar Racing Team, Sunswift. Built for practicality, speed, and endurance, eVe is the embodiment of Australian innovation and design. Originally created for the 2013 World Solar Challenge – Cruiser Class, eVe sets a new standard for sustainable transportation, offering high performance, efficiency, comfort and style – All powered directly from the sun. At the heart of eVe are her high-efficiency solar cells, sleek aerodynamics and carbon fibre body – making her the world’s fastest long-range electric car and Australia’s only road-registered solar car. Our vision is a world powered by renewables: please join us in our race towards a sustainable future as we create history and redefine the electric solar car.

PERFORMANCE

Although designed for practicality, eVe makes no compromises when it comes to performance. The car’s solar panels and lithium-ion battery pack offer an unprecedented range of 800km on a single charge. And with a top speed of 140km/h, eVe is the world’s fastest long-range electric car – owing to its sleek aerodynamics, low centre of gravity, and lightweight carbon-fibre shell.

PRACTICALITY

When it comes to practicality, no other solar car compares to eVe. With two seats and four wheels, you’d be forgiven for mistaking eVe for a conventional petrol-driven car. The battery pack and solar panels mean that ‘range anxiety’ is now a thing of the past, offering distances of 800km from a single charge –even when the sun is not shining. While also boasting a top speed of 140 km/h, well, we think this speaks for itself. After all, what’s more practical than getting to your destination on time?

SPECS

Seating capacity: 2

Range: 800 km

Top Speed: 140 km/h

Solar array output: 800 W

ENVIRONMENT

Many electric cars today are marketed under the façade of “Zero Emission” – forgetting that the electricity grid used to charge these vehicles is anything but zero emission, especially if you live in Australia. Sunswift eVe offers the option to be charged by a household power outlet, however relies primarily on solar energy delivered for free by the sun. Utilising the most advanced in Australian technology – high efficiency solar cells and electric motors – Sunwift eVe is a one-of-a-kind, environmentally-friendly electric supercar.
