The challenges involved in building and racing solar cars requires real-world problem solving skills and a strong work ethic. Sunswift teaches students to be self-reliant, self-motivated and to take leadership in applying their skills to acquire and apply new knowledge.

Sunswift consists of a variety of motivated students from a variety of backgrounds including:

  • Electrical, Mechanical and Photovoltaic Engineering
  • Business and Economics
  • Phd candidates, Honours students and UNSW Co-op Scholars from a range of disciplines.
