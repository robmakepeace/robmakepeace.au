OUR VISION

×

Sunswift is a team of aspirational students who believe in a future powered by renewables.

Having built Australia’s 1st passenger solar car, we believe solar cars demonstrate a tangible alternative to conventional petrol cars.

We compete against the best technical universities in the world and we race to win.

We represent the future of Australian engineering and the potential for innovative industry on an international stage.

We are students who are making an impact. Our goal is not just to win races. It is to change the public’s perception of how sustainable transport is an achievable reality.

We are racing towards a sustainable future.
